ABOUT

Hello, my name is David Quirke and I am a self-taught web designer, developer, and entrepreneur based in Ireland.

I always had a strong passion and curiosity for web technologies (CSS, HTML). My first exposure to the inner workings of website design and development was in 2013 when I undertook a course in Front End development in Cork City, Ireland.

The main area of my expertise is front end development and everything related with this side of web. HTML, CSS, JS (ES5,ES6), building small & medium Web Apps, animations and coding layouts.

Spending the past two years in the technical support industry in online payments companies like Stripe, I have been learning programming concepts and building web and mobile applications in my spare time.
